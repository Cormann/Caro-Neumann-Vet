Who I Am

A surgical specialist who comes to you

Carolina Neumann is a board-eligible veterinary surgeon with over a decade of advanced surgical training across university referral centres and specialist practices in Europe.

Specialising in soft tissue, thoracic, laparoscopic and endoscopic procedures, Carolina works on a freelance basis — travelling to referring practices to deliver specialist surgical care without the overhead of a referral centre.

Her approach prioritises minimally invasive technique, rapid patient recovery and close collaboration with the primary care team throughout every case.

Areas of Expertise

Surgical specialisms

Advanced procedures performed with precision across four core disciplines.

Soft Tissue Surgery

Comprehensive management of GI, hepatic, splenic, urogenital and oncological cases using refined open and laparoscopic approaches.

Thoracic Surgery

Lung lobectomy, pericardectomy and pleural disease management — delivered with the careful technique that chest surgery demands.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive access for ovariectomy, liver biopsy, prophylactic gastropexy and cryptorchid castration — reducing post-operative morbidity.

Endoscopy

Upper GI, bronchoscopy, rhinoscopy, cystoscopy and foreign body retrieval — diagnostic and interventional procedures with minimal invasiveness.

Why Refer to Carolina

Specialist care built around your practice

A referral to Carolina is seamless, collaborative and centred on the best outcome for your patient.

01

Minimally Invasive First

Wherever clinically appropriate, laparoscopic and endoscopic techniques are prioritised to reduce pain, hospital time and recovery burden for your patient.

02

Your Practice, Your Patient

Carolina operates at your premises using your team — no referral centre transfer needed. The patient stays in familiar, trusted hands.

03

Collaborative Approach

Every case involves thorough case discussion, clear pre-operative planning and a detailed written surgical report for your records and continuing care.

04

Rapid Recovery Focus

Enhanced recovery protocols and careful analgesia planning minimise post-operative discomfort and shorten hospitalisation times.

05

Transparent Communication

Owners are kept fully informed throughout. Clear consent discussions and honest prognosis form the foundation of every surgical plan.

06

Flexible Availability

Available across the region for both planned elective procedures and urgent surgical cases — contact Carolina to discuss scheduling.
